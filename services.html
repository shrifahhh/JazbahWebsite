Our Services

FOOD PROVISION SUPPLY SERVICES

As JESB's pioneer business since 2010, food provision supply remains the foundation of our oil and gas operations. We possess fully established onshore support facilities, cold-chain logistics, and credit facilities directly linked with reliable distributors and suppliers. Backed by our highly experienced management team, we guarantee the seamless and hygienic delivery of fresh, dry, and frozen Halal provisions to meet strict offshore regulatory standards

OFFSHORE CATERING & ASSOCIATED SERVICES

We provide comprehensive galley management and accommodation services designed to maintain high morale and well-being on offshore platforms. JESB manages everything from nutritious, culturally diverse menu planning to strict HSE-compliant housekeeping and laundry operations. Our marine-certified catering crews and camp bosses are meticulously trained in food safety management systems, ensuring top-tier hospitality even in the most demanding marine environments.

MANPOWER SUPPLY SERVICES

JESB offers rapid mobilization of highly qualified, certified offshore personnel to ensure your operations run without disruption. We specialize in vetting and deploying experienced camp bosses, executive chefs, professional stewards, and utility crews. Every candidate undergoes rigorous health screening, safety compliance checks, and possesses all necessary marine certifications (including BOSIET and HUET) to guarantee full readiness from day one.
