Health, Safety & Environment

Compliance & Risk

We plan and execute all operations to minimize safety risks and environmental hazards. Jazbah strictly complies with all applicable regulatory laws and international HSE standards by provisioning continuous resource training.

Transparent Communication

We maintain transparent, open communication lines with our employees, contractors, clients, and stakeholders to align our collective health, safety, and environmental expectations.

Continuous Improvement

Through rigorous periodic reviews of our HSE systems, we actively research and implement viable procedures to hit our ultimate operational target: Zero Incidents.

Emergency Readiness

We develop and maintain comprehensive emergency response strategies and active contingency resources to immediately mitigate unexpected operational disruptions.

Stop Work Authority

Individual safety takes priority over project schedules and economic targets. Every team member is fully empowered with 'Stop Work Authority' if any activity compromises HSE standards.
